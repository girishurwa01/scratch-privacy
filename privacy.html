Scratch Video Call Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 6, 2024

Scratch is a video call booking app created by a student for experimental purposes. We care about your privacy and want to be transparent about how we handle your data.

What data do we collect?

We collect the following data:

  • Your name and email address when you sign up for an account
  • Your device and browser information

How do we use your data?

We use your data to:

  • Provide you with our video call services
  • Improve our services and user experience

How do we protect your data?

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • We use secure servers and data centers to store your data
  • We limit access to your data to authorized personnel only

What are your rights?

You have the following rights:

  • The right to access and correct your data
  • The right to delete your data
